Executive Summary

The Revenue Gap We Are Here to Close

This engagement is designed for partner practices generating $2M–$20M ARR that have hit a revenue ceiling not explained by market conditions or technical capability. In our experience across 40 partner engagements, the ceiling is almost always structural — built into how the practice pitches, qualifies, and retains. This 12-week engagement identifies and closes the three highest-impact gaps in your current motion, leaving you with a live, independently operable outcome-led revenue system.

The Challenge

Where Most Partner Practices Are Losing Revenue

The following challenges are consistent across the partner practices we have assessed. They compound quietly — each one independently manageable, but together they create a ceiling that standard sales training will not break through.

Challenge 01
Feature-Led Pitching
Decision-makers respond to quantified outcomes, not product capability. Feature-led pitches stall at procurement and close at or below list price.
Challenge 02
No Outcome Evidence
Without packaged proof points from existing clients, every new sale starts from zero credibility. Deals take longer and require deeper discounting.
Challenge 03
Reactive Renewal Motion
Renewals treated as admin events result in late-stage churn that erases new logo gains. Without structured value reviews, retention is luck-dependent.
Challenge 04
Untapped Expansion ARR
Partners who close well but lack a structured expansion playbook leave an average of 30% of addressable ARR untouched per account per year.

According to Forrester, 68% of B2B technology buyers say the winning vendor was the one who helped them build the business case internally — not the one with the best product or the lowest price.

Our Approach

A Four-Phase Engagement With a Live Output at Each Stage

What this engagement looks like in practice: four phases over 12 weeks, each producing a deliverable your team can use immediately — not a deck to review and file.

Step 01
Discovery & Gap Mapping
We audit your current sales motion, pipeline data, and retention history to identify the three highest-impact structural gaps.
Step 02
Playbook & Proof Point Build
We produce your outcome story library, co-create your business case template, and rebuild your pitch structure around quantified buyer outcomes.
Step 03
Team Activation & Live Practicum
We run your AEs through the new motion using live deals in your active pipeline as the practicum — not simulated scenarios.
Output
Live Revenue Motion
A fully operational outcome-led sales and retention motion, with a 90-day performance baseline and a handoff pack your team owns independently.
